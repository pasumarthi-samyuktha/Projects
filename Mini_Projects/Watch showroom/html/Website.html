A watch is a portable timepiece intended to be carried or worn
by a person. It is designed to keep a consistent movement despite the
motions caused by the person's activities. A wristwatch is designed to be
worn around the wrist,attached by a watch strap or other type of bracelet,
including metal bands, leather straps, or any other kind of bracelet.
A pocket watch is designed for a person to carry in a pocket, often
attached to a chain.
